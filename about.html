Six interesting things about OpenAQ

  • OpenAq shifts the latitude and longitude coordinates (0,0) to _
  • Although the area around Beijing China has unhealthy air quality, moving a few miles out of Beijing have fairly good to moderate air quality
  • Around the Beijing area, readings for carbon-monoxide (co), nitrogen-dioxide (no2), and sulfur-dioxide (so2) are mostly good. Only pm25, pm10 and ozone (o3) appear in particularly unhealthy amount.
